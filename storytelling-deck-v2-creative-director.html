01 / 14

The Tension

Luxury agents close $50M+ a year.

Their AI assistant runs 24/7 in the background — optimizing listings, nurturing leads, writing content. Silently.

They have no idea if it's helping or hurting.

02 / 14

The Cost

Two failure modes.

Blind faith

Agent trusts the AI completely. Doesn't catch errors. Reputation risk on every automated action.

Constant checking

Agent second-guesses every move. Defeats the purpose of automation entirely.

Black Box Information Overload
Signal to Action
03 / 14

The Thesis

"Show just enough reasoning to earn the next action."

The sweet spot between black box and information overload. Not more data — the right data, at the right moment.

04 / 14
01

Design Principle

Lead with the action,
not the data.

Agents don't want to interpret charts. They want to know: what should I do next, and why should I trust it?

Action first. Evidence on demand.

05 / 14
02

Design Principle

Build trust through
track record.

A confidence score means nothing on day one. Trust is earned by showing outcomes — "I suggested this last time, and here's what happened."

Past performance builds future confidence.

06 / 14
03

Design Principle

Progressive disclosure.

Surface the summary. Let curious agents dig deeper. Most won't — and that's the point.

Respect attention. Reward curiosity.
