Beach Guide

Stunning Scenery & Calm Waters
Playa Conchal
Famous for its sand made of crushed shells, clear turquoise water ideal for swimming and snorkeling.
Playa San Juanillo
A secluded gem where two white sand beaches meet, offering calm, clear waters for relaxing.
Surfing & Lively Atmosphere
Playa Tamarindo
Bustling surf town with consistent waves perfect for learning, plus restaurants, shops, and nightlife.
Playa Grande
Rugged, expansive beach known for surfing and as a nesting site for leatherback turtles.
Families & Relaxed Luxury
Playa Flamingo
Powdery white sand, calm waters, and upscale marina with dining and tours.
Playa Hermosa
Gentle waves, excellent for swimming, kayaking, and watersports with great diving nearby.
Other Favorites
Playa Avellanas
Rustic vibe with excellent surf breaks.
Playa Samara
Beautiful crescent-shaped bay with calm waters, popular with families.
Good to Know

Travel Notes

Use Waze for all navigation. Google Maps is less reliable in Costa Rica.
Car rental: Adobe — ref #1318719 under Ashley. Free 2nd driver and free cooler.
Try chili guaro shots — local specialty. "kai-pee-REEN-yah" for the adventurous.
Ferry: Paquera → Puntarenas — book through QuickPayCR before departure day.
TSA PreCheck expires 9/8/2028.
