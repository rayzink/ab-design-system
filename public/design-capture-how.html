1·Human
Designer speaks
Hit record and describe what you want to build. The Web Speech API transcribes in real time. Talk naturally — the system extracts ideas from your stream of consciousness. No prompts to write, no forms to fill.
REC
10:32:04 I want a hero section with a bold headline and a call to action button
10:32:11 also a nav bar with the logo on the left and links on the right
10:32:18 maybe a pricing table with three tiers...
2·AI
Ideas extracted from speech
Every 10 seconds, the transcript buffer is sent to Claude. The AI parses natural language into discrete, buildable UI ideas — each with a title, description, component type, and the exact quote that triggered it. Filler talk is ignored.

3·System
Tokens constrain generation
Before generating code, the system wraps every prompt with your full design token set. Colors, fonts, spacing, radii. The AI cannot use values outside this set. Output is on-brand by construction, not by review.

4·Output
Components render live
Claude returns raw HTML. It drops into a sandboxed iframe — a live, interactive preview. The designer sees the actual rendered component, not a code diff. Each card on the board gets a thumbnail captured from this render.

Summary
Who does what
Designer
Talks through ideas. Reviews output. Refines with plain language. Approves to page. Directs — doesn't code.
Design System
The constraint layer. Wraps every generation with tokens. Makes AI output safe to ship.
Claude AI
Extracts ideas from speech. Generates HTML. Applies refinements. Executes — doesn't decide.
The insight
Voice is the most natural design tool. Designers already talk through ideas in reviews, crits, and standups. Design Capture turns that conversation into components — no translation step, no ticket, no handoff. The design system ensures every output is on-brand by default.
